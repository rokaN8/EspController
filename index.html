Instructions:

  1. Click "Connect to ESP32" to scan for devices
  2. Select "ESP32-LED-Controller" from the list
  3. Click LED grid squares to cycle colors
  4. Click "Send Configuration" to update LED strip
  5. Use "Clear All" to reset all LEDs to off

Requirements:

  • Chrome or Edge browser
  • HTTPS or localhost
  • ESP32 with FastLED library
  • WS2811 LED strip (30 LEDs)
